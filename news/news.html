October 2025

  • IIIT-Bangalore will host two major international conferences — ATVA 2025 and APLAS 2025 — from 27 to 31 October 2025. The five-day event will feature multiple invited talks, paper presentations, tutorials, workshops, and a NEIR track, bringing together experts and researchers from around the world.

2025 Updates

  • Prof. Meenakshi D’Souza has been elected as the President of ACM India for the term 1 July 2025 to 30 June 2027.
  • IIIT-Bangalore will host the Automated Technology for Verification and Analysis (ATVA) 2025 conference. Prof. Sujit Kumar Chakrabarti and Prof. Meenakshi D'Souza are serving as the Organizing Chairs for the event.
  • Prof. Meenakshi D'Souza is also a member of the Technical Program Committee (TPC) for ATVA 2025.
  • Additionally, APLAS 2025, which is collocated with ATVA 2025, includes Prof. Meenakshi D'Souza on its Technical Program Committee.
  • For more details, visit the ATVA 2025 Organizing Committee page.
  • Prof. Sujit Kumar Chakrabarti was invited as a speaker at TEDxDSATM, an independently organized TED event held at Dayananda Sagar Academy of Technology and Management, Bengaluru, on May 16, 2025.
  • His talk, titled “A Humorous Take on Indian Academia,” offered a candid and engaging perspective on the academic landscape in India.
  • View TEDx Event Page
  • Prof. Meenakshi D’Souza has been conferred the ACM India Outstanding Contributions to Computing Education (OCCE) Award for the year 2024, in recognition of her impactful work in computing education.
  • Ms. Ameena K Ashraf (PH2018001), under the supervision of Prof. Meenakshi D’Souza, successfully presented her thesis oral examination on May 12, 2025, titled “Testing and Program Analysis based Verification of Programs for Industrial Robots.”
  • Mr. Ganesha (Roll No. PH2019003), a research scholar under the supervision of Prof. Sujit Kumar Chakrabarti, delivered his State of the Art Seminar on May 8, 2025 on “A Formal Verification Approach to Safeguard Controller Variables from Single Event Upsets”
